About Me

Hi, my name is Jordan Benbelaid and thank you for visiting my website. The purpose of this project is below, so please take a moment to take a look at what I am trying to achieve. But first, a little about me! I graduated from Brunel University last year with a Computer Science degree. My hobbies tend to include anything sport related. I was in the 1st Rugby team at Brunel and before that I played at County and local level. I was also a Tae Kwon Do Instructor, assisting at my local TKD school. Now I play Basketball and five-a-side. I have always demonstrated a particular strength in Maths, which not only helped me with my programming but in the past has earned me some money through tutoring younger students through their exams. I really enjoy gaming, although I had to reduce this while in University! I am a particular fan of League of Legends and sports games. While I have been looking for work, I have also gone back to reading, which was one of my favourite things to do a few years back. I particularly enjoy the adventure/mystery genre. I have been brushing up on my skills by revising some of the things I learnt, such as HTML, CSS and C#. I have recently been commissioned to build two websites, which will be my next upcoming projects. I am not taking on too many at a time, as I am hoping to be in work soon and would still want to commit to completing these for the clients.

Why This Website?

After finishing my Computer Science degree at Brunel, I took some time out to travel and help my dad to complete a home extension project (see pictures below!). I then joined Sparta Global in January where I trained for three months in C# in preparation for the few placement interviews they had lined up for the end of March 2020. All the positions literally fell through in the space of a couple of weeks, due to Covid. Fast forward a few months and it has been quite difficult to get into a developer/programming role, so I have decided to be more proactive and do some self promotion! I built this website from scratch using mainly HTML and CSS. It has been built to showcase the following: My commitment to being proactive and doing whatever it takes to achieve my goals My CV and contact details My programming skills and portfolio as I add to this Click here to see the website code on Git Hub. I would welcome any feedback or pointers on improvements I can make on the website and will be continually improving it.
